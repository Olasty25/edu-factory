 # Biomy Świata - Przykłady

## Quiz

1. Which biome is characterized by tall trees, high rainfall, and a warm climate?
   A. Desert
   B. Tropical Rainforest
   C. Tundra
   D. Grassland

   Answer: B

2. What type of biome is the Taiga?
   A. Forest
   B. Desert
   C. Mountain
   D. Grassland

   Answer: A
