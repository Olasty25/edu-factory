 # Quiz - Test yourself on UN's goals and structure

1. What does UN stand for?
   A. United Nations
   B. Union of Nations
   C. Universal Nations
   D. None of the above

   Answer: A. United Nations

2. When was the UN established?
   A. 1945
   B. 1950
   C. 1939
   D. 1955

   Answer: A. 1945

3. How many main organs does the UN have?
   A. 4
   B. 5
   C. 6
   D. 3

   Answer: B. 5
